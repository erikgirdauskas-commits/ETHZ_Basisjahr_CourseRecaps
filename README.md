# ETHZ_Basisjahr_CourseRecaps
A public repository to store my semester recaps of each Basisjahr subject for the Computer Science bachelor at ETH Zürich.
Recaps for Analysis I and Algorithmen & Wahrscheinlichkeit are actively being updated and will be up to date the day before each minitest.
The recaps for Digital Design & Computer Architecture and Parallele Programmierung will be made during the Lernphase as well as the ones for Lineare Algebra, Algorithmen & Datenstrukturen, and Einführung in die Programmierung.

# Content and Style
The recaps are handwritten and feature all formal definitions taught also explaining the intuition behind key concepts. This way, you have access to just the essentials to add to your cheat sheet in the Lernphase, but if you need an explanation of a current topic or how to apply it, this is also given via diagrams, example proofs, and occasionally, topical meme templates. For the current recaps, many examples are sourced from the lectures and my labs.
